About Camping Hub

Who We Are?

We are a company which helps people book for camping trips all over India. We tie up with local camp organizors and display their camps on our website. This helps both the camp organizers to get customers from all over India, and the customers, who can find all types of camps being organized in India on one platform.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make it easy for the customers to book for an adventure camping trip in India. We want to connect each and every camp organizer in our country to the online platform. This will increase the intra-country tourism and indirectly help the country's development.

Message From The Managing Director

Our company is dedicated to provide the best booking service to our customers. We, at camp hub believe in keeping good relationships with our customers which are fruitful and long lasting.
